Carmen de la Legua, Peru

The Carmen de La Legua-Reynoso District is one of the seven districts that make up the constitutional province of Callao in Peru . It borders the San Martín de Porres District , Lima Province , to the north; the Lima District , also part of the province of the same name , to the east ; and the Callao District to the south and west
